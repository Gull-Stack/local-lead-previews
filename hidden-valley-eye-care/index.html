Meet Dr. Jason Mackay

Dr. Mackay completed his undergraduate studies at Brigham Young University-Idaho with a BS in Exercise Physiology before earning his Doctor of Optometry degree from Southern California College of Optometry.

With over 15 years of experience serving the Draper community, Dr. Mackay is passionate about providing personalized eye care that goes beyond just checking your vision. He takes the time to understand your lifestyle, work environment, and visual needs to create customized solutions.

Whether you need a routine exam, contact lens fitting, or treatment for an eye condition, Dr. Mackay and his dedicated team are here to help you see your best.
